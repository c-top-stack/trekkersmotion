Have questions?

Do you guarantee this will help me grow?
Every successful YouTube channel is different, but they all have one thing in common - they consistently make great content for their community. While we can't guarantee growth, we can guarantee that we'll help you make great content for your channel.
Will you film my videos?
That's practically the only thing we don't do! We can't film your video for you but we can help you setup the perfect studio, dial-in your recording equipment, and help you with your recording process.
How long does a video take?
It depends on a bunch of things! Are we helping you generate ideas and write the script? Or are you only looking for TrekkersMotion to edit the video and handle packaging? On average videos can take 2 days and up to a week or more depending on complexity.
Can I do consulting calls with Ali Abdaal?
No, unfortunately not. But you can do regular consulting calls with our team! We might not have 4.5m subscribers like Ali, but we know his entire playbook.
Why wouldn’t I just hire my own team?
Managing a team is tough. Making sure they're always getting your brand, strategy, and quality right is even harder. With TrekkersMotion! you don't have to worry about any of that. It's all managed for you with zero headaches.
Do you only make YouTube videos?
We're specialized in YouTube, but sometimes people will publish our long-form videos in other places. It's up to you.
